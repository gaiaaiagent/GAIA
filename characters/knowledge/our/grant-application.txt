Briefly describe your project activities in plain language. This is an important section as it will be used in summary documents to describe your project at various review stages (maximum of 1,000 characters including spaces).

Awaken the Dragon is an AI-powered educational platform that transforms climate resilience education through personalized learning pathways and real-time adaptation strategies. The project implements proven AI technologies across 9 interactive learning kiosks at OUR Ecovillage, creating a scalable model for climate education and wellness programming. Building on existing partnerships with educational institutions and validated through years of on-site programming, this commercialization-ready system integrates predictive modeling, adaptive learning algorithms, and engagement metrics to deliver measurable outcomes in climate literacy and community resilience. The platform will generate revenue through licensing, professional development programs, and eco-tourism, while creating sustainable jobs in BC's growing AI sector.

By bringing together software developers, educational institutions, and sustainability organizations, we're establishing an innovative product development ecosystem that strengthens regional competitiveness.


Product Creation & Commercialization emerges through development of market-ready AI learning platforms integrated with physical learning spaces. Our AI-enhanced educational tools adapt to individual and group learning needs while gathering valuable data on climate resilience education efficacy. The scalable products include customizable learning modules, assessment tools, and engagement metrics that can be licensed to educational institutions and organizations across Canada and internationally.


Cross-Sector Collaboration happens through deep integration with academic partners like UVic for ongoing R&D and validation of our approaches. We're building bridges between K-12 educators, corporate training providers, and Indigenous knowledge holders to create comprehensive climate resilience education programs. This collaborative approach ensures our products meet real market needs while advancing BC's position as a leader in AI-enhanced education.
